About Me

I'm currently a self-taught developer based in Hamilton, NJ pursuing job opportunities. My personal philosophy is that I am a student for life and for me this means I am always learning and always aiming to better myself. In particular, I like to utilize what I learn from online courses and apply that learning to my personal projects such as making a website, writing code to convert scraped data into usable excel sheets, or writing code that aids my daily life like a BMI (body mass index) calculator. In brief, I am determined to join a company where I can interface my skills with other like-minded individuals and where we can grow to become something bigger than ourselves. Programming can be difficult at times with seemingly unsolvable problems but it is in that moment of clarity when the solution is discovered that makes the struggle worth the while, and it is why I am proud to be a developer.
