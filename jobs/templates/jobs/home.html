About Me

I'm a self-taught developer based in Hamilton, NJ currently pursuing an entry level developer position. My personal philosophy is that I am a student for life and for me this means always searching for learning opportunities. After realizing I was unhappy with my current job direction, I began searching for a new career path that would allow me to exercise my philosophy more often. After running my first program, I knew I had found the right path.

The first couple of projects I created were simple ranging from a Body Mass Index calculator to a web scraper. Since then, I have transitioned to more complex apps such as my personal website, a weather based app connected to a geolocation API and a weather API, and more recently a chat-app where the users can join private chat rooms.

Ultimately, I am determined to join a company where I can synthesize my skills with other like-minded individuals to create remarkable code. Programming can be difficult at times with seemingly complex problems, but the feeling of success when discovering a solution drives me to work harder and harder.
